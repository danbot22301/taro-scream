The Bird Life: How To Scream

The Beauty of Noise

Weaponizing Annoyance

Its not easy to get your point across if you can't talk! Luckily, birds have found a solution to this conundrum; in the event you want or need something, just start screaming your little feathered head off. Hungry? Scream. Scared? Scream, Tired? Scream, and then close your eyes. While this is a primative method of communication, one cannot overstate its usefulness once both parties understand the message. Noisemaking isn't just an effective tool however, its also a lot of fun! nothing can cast off boredom like making as great a raucous as is possible.
